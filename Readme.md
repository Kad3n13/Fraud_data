<h1>Data was collected and structured by the Fraud team. This dataset consists of payments from various customers made in different periods and amounts. The feature columns include:</h1>
<h6>

    Step: This feature represents the month from the start of the simulation. The steps represent four months that the simulation ran virtually.
        0: May
        1: June
        2: July
        3: August
    Customer: Customer ID
    Age: Categorised age
        0.0: <= 18
        1.0: 19 - 25
        2.0: 26 - 35
        3.0: 36 - 45
        4.0: 46 - 55
        5.0: 56 - 65
    Gender: Gender of the customer
        F: Female
        M: Male
    PostcodeOrigin: The postcode of origin/source.
    Merchant: The merchant's ID. 
    Category: Category of the purchase. 
    Amount: Amount of the purchase.
    Fraud: Target variable that shows if the transaction is fraudulent - 1 or non-fraudulent - 0.
